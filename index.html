About us

Who we are

ZAF Pharmaceutical and Healthcare Ltd. is a pioneering pharmaceutical importation and distribution company committed to improving healthcare access in Nigeria and Africa. 

Our Mission

To enhance healthcare delivery by ensuring the availability of high-quality pharmaceutical products and services through innovation, integrity, and excellence.

Our Vision

To be the most trusted and preferred pharmaceutical and healthcare company in Africa, recognized for quality, efficiency, and ethical business practices.

🚀 Our Story

Founded in 2019 as a Patent and Proprietary Medicine Vendor in Sokoto, ZAF Pharmaceutical began by serving communities with essential medications. Today, we serve over 500 healthcare clients across Nigeria, and we’re expanding our footprint across the nation and into other African markets.
From humble beginnings to a nationwide network — our journey reflects our commitment to better healthcare for all.

💼 What We Do

IMPORTATION

We source and import pharmaceuticals and medical equipment from certified global manufacturers, ensuring international safety and quality standards.

DISTRIBUTION

We deliver timely and reliable pharmaceutical products to hospitals, pharmacies, wholesalers, and other stakeholders across Nigeria.

MARKETING

We promote high-quality pharmaceutical and healthcare products with targeted outreach to healthcare providers and consumers.

HEALTHCARE SOLUTIONS

We provide tailored healthcare support services to institutions and individuals — combining innovation, expertise, and care.

✅ Compliance & Quality Assurance

At ZAF, we uphold the highest standards of quality and operate in full compliance with national and international pharmaceutical regulations. All our products go through rigorous checks to ensure safety, efficacy, and regulatory approval.
